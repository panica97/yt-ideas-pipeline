## Round 1: Discovery

**Q:** List ALL the distinct trading strategies, entry methods, or trading systems mentioned in these sources.

**A:** 5 AI-Generated Entry Methods identified:
1. **Volume Weighted Reversal** - Uses VWAP and ATR to identify price reversals where close is compared to VWAP or previous price action
2. **Shakeout Fakeout Entry** - Compares current bar's close to previous bar's close to identify price action "shakeouts"
3. **Market Maker Stop Hunt** - Identifies when price hits new low over lookback period, assuming market makers have "run the stops"
4. **Hidden RSI Exhaustion Point Entry** - Looks for bullish divergence where RSI makes higher low while price makes lower low
5. **Volume Imbalance Reversal** - Identifies "quiet positioning" by looking for bars with high volume but little movement between open and close

Note: Shakeout Fakeout and Market Maker Stop Hunt were merged for testing (identical logic).

## Round 2: Rules Extraction

### Volume Weighted Reversal
- **LONG:** Close > Previous Close (simplified from original VWAP comparison)
- **SHORT:** Mirror of long (Close < Previous Close)
- **EXIT v1:** Stop and Reverse (always in market)
- **EXIT v2:** Exit after 20 bars
- **INDICATORS:** VWAP (still in system), ATR (Length optimized over 7 iterations)
- **Bar comparisons:** Current Close vs Previous Close

### Shakeout Fakeout / Market Maker Stop Hunt
- **LONG:** Low of current bar == Lowest Low of lookback period (corrected from Low < Lowest Low)
- **SHORT:** High of current bar == Highest High of lookback period
- **EXIT v1:** Stop and Reverse
- **EXIT v2:** Exit after 20 bars
- **INDICATORS:** Lowest Low / Highest High function, lookback length optimized (9 iterations, values 5-40)

### Hidden RSI Exhaustion Point Entry
- **LONG:** Price makes lower low AND RSI makes higher low AND RSI < threshold
- **SHORT:** Price makes higher high AND RSI makes lower high (bearish divergence)
- **EXIT v1:** Stop and Reverse
- **EXIT v2:** Exit after 20 bars
- **INDICATORS:** RSI (period not specified, 14 assumed). Threshold values tested: 50, 60, 70, 80, 90
- **Divergence pattern:** RSI "deep low", bounce, then "higher low" while price makes "lower low"

### Volume Imbalance Reversal
- **LONG:** Volume > 2 * Average Volume(Length) AND Abs(Close - Open) < 0.2 * ATR AND Close > Open
- **SHORT:** Same volume/body conditions AND Close < Open
- **EXIT v1:** Stop and Reverse
- **EXIT v2:** Exit after 20 bars
- **INDICATORS:** Volume, Average Volume (Length optimized, 9 iterations), ATR
