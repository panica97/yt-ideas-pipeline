## Turn 1: Discovery

**Q:** List ALL the distinct trading strategies, entry methods, or trading systems mentioned in these sources. For each one, give: (1) a short name, (2) which video/source it comes from, (3) a one-sentence summary of the core idea.

**A:** Based on the sources provided, here are the distinct trading entry methods mentioned by Kevin Davey, all of which were generated using AI:

* **Volume Weighted Reversal** - Uses a Volume Weighted Average Price (VWAP) calculation and Average True Range (ATR) to trigger trades based on how the current close compares to the previous close.
* **Shakeout Fake Out / Market Maker Stop Hunt** - Uses a look-back length to identify when the price hits a new low, suggesting "stops were run" before the market reverses.
* **Hidden RSI Exhaustion Point Entry** - Seeks to identify hidden strength by looking for instances where the RSI makes a higher low while the market price is simultaneously making a lower low.
* **Volume Imbalance Reversal** - Triggers when high volume occurs on a bar with a very small price range (close minus open) relative to the ATR, based on the theory that large players are quietly positioning themselves.

**A:**

* **INDICATORS:** Lowest Low / Highest High with lookback length N (9 iterations, e.g. 5,10,15,...,40)

## Turn 4: Hidden RSI Exhaustion - Rules

**Q:** For the strategy 'Hidden RSI Exhaustion Point Entry', give me the EXACT trading rules...

**A:**
* **ENTRY LONG:** Price makes lower low + RSI makes higher low (divergence) + RSI below threshold
* **ENTRY SHORT:** Mirror - Price higher high + RSI lower high + RSI above threshold
* **EXIT V1:** Stop and Reverse
* **EXIT V2:** Time exit after 20 bars
* **INDICATORS:** RSI (period not specified), RSI threshold (5 iterations: 50,60,70,80,90)
* **TEMPORAL:** Compares current bar Low/RSI to previous bar Low/RSI for divergence pattern (deep low -> bounce -> higher low)

## Turn 5: Volume Imbalance Reversal - Rules

**Q:** For the strategy 'Volume Imbalance Reversal', give me the EXACT trading rules...

**A:**
* **ENTRY LONG:** Volume > 2 * Average Volume AND abs(Close - Open) < 0.2 * ATR + directional filter
* **ENTRY SHORT:** Symmetric with mirror directional filter
* **EXIT V1:** Stop and Reverse
* **EXIT V2:** Time exit after 20 bars
* **INDICATORS:** Volume (upticks in TradeStation), Average Volume (9 iterations), ATR
* **NOTE:** Original AI code was non-directional, Davey added symmetric directionality
