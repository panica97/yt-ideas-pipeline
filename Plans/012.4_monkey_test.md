## Goal

Implement the Monkey Test — a statistical robustness test that generates N simulations of random entries on real OHLC data, computes performance metrics for each, and positions the real strategy within the resulting distribution to produce a p-value. This answers: "Could a monkey generating random trades have achieved equal or better results than my strategy?"
